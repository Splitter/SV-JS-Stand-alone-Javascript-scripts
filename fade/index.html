SV Fade Menu fading background menu script

> SV Fade Menu

This script will allow you to create a fancy fade transition between portions of a sprite as a user hovers over a menu item. It is completely stand alone requiring absolutely no third party frameworks or libraries. And is less then 1kb minified.

Examples:

Horizontal Fade Menu

The Code:

fademenu.init('navigation',NewX,NewY,Speed,Steps);


Must be called on window.onload and not when the dom loaded, it must be initialized after the images have loaded.
